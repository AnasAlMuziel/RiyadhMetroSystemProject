About Riyadh Metro

Riyadh Metro is a state-of-the-art public transportation system designed to connect the city seamlessly, offering a fast, reliable, and eco-friendly way to travel. With three modern lines and 35 stations, the metro network aims to reduce traffic congestion, lower carbon emissions, and enhance the quality of life for residents and visitors alike.

Our mission is to provide a safe, efficient, and comfortable commuting experience, ensuring that every journey is smooth and enjoyable. Riyadh Metro is more than just a transportation system—it’s a step toward a smarter, more sustainable future for the city of Riyadh.
